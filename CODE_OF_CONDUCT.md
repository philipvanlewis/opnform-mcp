# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) v2.1.

In short: be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion constructive. Harassment, discrimination, and personal attacks are not tolerated.

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private [security advisory](https://github.com/philipvanlewis/opnform-mcp/security/advisories/new) (it's a private channel to the maintainers) or contact the maintainer directly. Reports are handled confidentially.
